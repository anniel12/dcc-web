DIRECTIONS

The Design Careers Fair will be held at the Art Annex.
Address: Hutchison Dr, Davis, CA 95616

Art Annex is located behind the UC Davis Art Building and to the left of the walkway towards Mrak Hall.

Art Annex 107 (Main Room) will be used as the main stage for welcoming guest companies and UC Davis students. Art Annex 112 (Seminar Room) will be hosting the speaker seminar where professors, alumni, and guest speakers will share their stories.

PARKING LOTS

Visiting guests will be given a ONE-DAY visitors parking permit, allowing them to park in the C permit lots.

North Entry Parking Structure (by Hickey Gym & near MU)

Visitor Parking Lot 5 (on Arboretum Dr & Old Davis Rd)

South Entry Parking Structure (next to Manetti Shrem Museum)

ROUTES

Take route I-80 E from San Francisco and route I-80 W from Sacramento.
